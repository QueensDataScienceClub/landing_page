Queen's Data Science Club

The Queen's Data Science Club is a student-run group intended to create a collaborative environment for students at Queen's University to discuss and solve interesting data-oriented problems. The club is open to both undergraduate and graduate students, and will touch on data exploration and processing, visualization, statistical analysis, and introductory machine learning. We presume no prior knowledge other than basic Python. QDSC meets every [date] at [time] in [location]. We'll post the week's topic or activity below, as well as on our Facebook page. Feel free to drop by at any point in the semester, even just to say hi and check out what we're up to!
