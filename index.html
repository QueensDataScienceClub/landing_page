Queen's Data Science Club

The Queen's Data Science Club is a student-run group intended to create a collaborative environment for students at Queen's University to discuss and solve interesting data-science problems. The club is open to both undergraduate and graduate students, and will touch on data exploration and processing, visualization, statistical analysis, and machine learning. We'll be working on various Kaggle datasets throughout the year.

QDSC meets every Sunday at 4:00PM in the Goodwin Hall labs (254 or 247). Keep up with our most recent events on our facebook page or down below. We presume no prior knowledge other than basic Python, feel free to drop by at any point in the semester, even just to say hi and check out what we're up to!
